Coffee Shop Senja Sore is a tranquil haven tucked away in a bustling city. With its soothing ambiance, natural decor, and warm lighting, it offers a peaceful retreat for coffee lovers. The menu boasts a diverse range of premium coffees, meticulously prepared by skilled baristas, ensuring a delightful and flavorful experience. Indulge in their selection of pastries and desserts while relaxing in the comfortable seating areas, providing a cozy and inviting atmosphere.

To apply for a job as a barista at Coffee Shop Senja Sore, the specific requirements include:
1. Previous experience in the food and beverage industry, preferably in a cafe or coffee shop setting.
2. Strong knowledge and passion for coffee, including the ability to prepare espresso-based beverages and familiarity with different brewing methods.
3. Excellent customer service skills with the ability to engage with customers, take orders, and provide a positive and welcoming experience.
4. Effective communication and interpersonal skills to work well with a team and collaborate in a fast-paced environment.

As a barista at a coffee shop, your main responsibilities include preparing and serving high-quality coffee beverages, engaging with customers in a friendly manner, and ensuring smooth operations. You will operate coffee equipment, handle cash transactions, and maintain cleanliness and organization in the shop. Collaborating with the team, staying updated on industry trends, and adhering to health and safety regulations are also important aspects of the role.
